The OpenNV Project

Disclaimer: OpenNV is in the preliminary stage of development. Don't get your hopes up!

The OpenNV project is a collection of projects related to making Fallout: New Vegas mod development easier and allowing the game and it's mod content to be played on additional platforms. All OpenNV Projects are licensed under the 3-Clause BSD License.

OpenNV itself (part of, the OpenNV Project) is an engine recreation of the games Fallout: New Vegas and Fallout 3. It's written in C# and plans on using MOGRE for rendering.

OpenNVCK is a mod SDK for Fallout: New Vegas, aimed at being easier to use and less error-prone than the New Vegas Garden of Eden Creation Kit (GECK).

Current Progress

Current development efforts are focused on OpenNVCK. Code will be committed once the OpenNVCK is in a usable state.

Major Challenges

Current major challeneges are:

  • Parsing middleware formats used by New Vegas and making them usable ingame, in order of most-to-least expected difficulty:
    • FaceGen
    • SpeedTree
    • Bink Video
  • General lack of documentation on formats used by the the Fallout 3/New Vegas engine.
