The OpenNV project is a collection of projects related to making Fallout: New Vegas mod development easier and allowing the game and it's mod content to be played on additional platforms.

OpenNV itself (part of, the OpenNV Project) is an engine recreation of the games Fallout: New Vegas and Fallout 3.

OpenNVCK is an SDK for Fallout: New Vegas, aimed at being easier to use and less error-prone than the New Vegas Garden of Eden Creation Kit (GECK).
